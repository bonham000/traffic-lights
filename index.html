This page uses JavaScript to simulate the traffic light logic of a four-way intersection.

Some notes:

Clicking the buttons represents a car stopping at the light and will trigger the light to change appropriately. Right now, there is no delay and the change occurs immediately.

Currently both lanes on one street are allowed to turn left if a request is made at either. Many traffic lights have this behavior, but this could also be modified so only the lane that requested to turn left turns left (traffic going straight in that lane could continue).
